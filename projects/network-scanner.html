Custom PC Builds & Hardware Optimization

I work with people to turn an idea for a computer into a system that actually fits their needs. Whether the goal is gaming, productivity, creative work, or a balanced everyday setup, I focus on understanding what they want, what their budget allows, and how to build the best possible system around those priorities.

Approach
Consult first, then build around real goals instead of just specs.
Focus
Performance, reliability, upgrade flexibility, and clean hardware choices.
Value
A system that feels intentional, balanced, and worth the investment.

What I Do

Every build starts with a conversation. I like understanding how the system will actually be used, what kind of performance matters most, what visual style the person wants, and where the budget needs to be flexible or protected. From there, I translate that vision into a practical parts list and a build plan that makes sense.

  • Consult with users to understand goals, budget, and intended use case
  • Choose parts that balance performance, compatibility, and long-term value
  • Assemble and configure systems with attention to thermals and reliability
  • Troubleshoot issues involving hardware, BIOS settings, drivers, or stability
  • Refine builds so they match both the vision and the real-world constraints
What I Can Work With
  • Gaming PCs and performance-focused systems
  • Productivity and workstation-oriented builds
  • Budget-conscious systems with smart upgrade paths
  • Component compatibility and hardware planning
  • BIOS / UEFI setup, drivers, and optimization
  • Thermals, airflow, cable management, and clean assembly
